Time to get rid of a sacred cow!

As you will know, we don't believe in bonuses at Incentro. Time to break that rule (sorry board!).

For the first time ever we will hand out a bonus for great performance. We convinced Google to invest $10.000,- in our sales efforts throughout the company.

This will help us all to go to Ibiza!

but why ?

A lot of our solutions runs on legacy infrastructures (like on-premise hardware, AWS, Azure or AS/400’s) just because ‘this is easy’. Believe us: working with Google Cloud Platform is just as easy. That’s why we would like every incentronaut to consider GCP as the defacto standard for hosting customer projects.

Ibiza here we come!
